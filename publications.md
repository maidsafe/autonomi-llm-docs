## Google Tech Talk (2008)

On 14 June 2008, David Irvine presented a paper at Google's Seattle campus outlining the core concepts that would become MaidSafe and later the Autonomi Network. This predates Bitcoin's white paper by several months.

The talk covered:
- Anonymous/self authentication
- Self-healing network (perpetual data)
- Globally distributed Public Key Infrastructure (PKI)
- Self encryption
- Duplicate prevention

- [Watch on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fLA77zxk-vA)
